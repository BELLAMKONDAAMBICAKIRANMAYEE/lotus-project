LOTUS Tile Adhesive material is formulated uniquely to fix tiles. It is also suitable for foxing diferent construction materias interiors and exteriors. It has a high work ability and thus makes the application very easy. It provides from wet conditions To accommodate normal floor movements this tile adhesive provides a good range of flexibility. It is very important to take precautionary methods by avoiding wet tiles before fixing. It does not leave any stains on the tiles hence giving a fine fishing to the surface.

Usage Instructions :

  • Prepare the surface by cleaning all dirt/ dust and make the surface slightly moist using water.
  • Let the mixture stand for 10 minutes to give enough time for the additives to dissolve. After 10 minutes, mix it well. The mixture is ready for use.
  • Apply the mixed material with trowel 2 to 3 mm thickness. Take the tile and press it firmly onto the surface such that it gets locked into place
  • It is very important to ensure that the fresh mortar covers the backside of the whole tile.
