Ready Wall Plaster

Technicaldata Specifications :
Water Demand 23.6%
FORM Dry Powder
Coverage 40-60 Sq.ft/40 kg bag at 6-10 mm
Thickness 6-12 mm
Pot Life Approx 120 minutes at 30C
Curing Water 2-3 times a day for 7 days
Bulk Density Approx 1.8 Kg / ltr
Compressive Strength >4 MPA after 28 days
Flexural Strength(28 days) 3.70 N/mm2

It is not easy to switch to ready made plastering mix leaving the traditional plastering method behind. LOTUS ready wall plaster has a lasting impact on this changeover with its high quality. It has very good bonding properties that adheres to the base very strongly. The surface appears smooth and glossy. It is suitable for diferent plastering substrates such as AAC blocks, CLC blocks and cement bricks. It can be eficiently used on bricks, blocks and concrete walls. The extra benefit is that it can also be used on inner and outer walls.

Usage Instructions :

  • Prepare the surface by cleaning all dust and make the surface slightly moist using water.
  • Mix 5 kgs of powder with 1 Itr of water (Approx) and stir until wer get uniform mix
  • Apply the mixed material 6 mm to 12 mm thickness.
  • Use the material within 60 min mixing
  • For better result, cure the wall with water 2 to 3 times per day for 7 days.
